# Population Health Analysis
## Healthcare Spending & Life Expectancy Across Nations

## Overview
Analyzed healthcare spending and life expectancy trends across 6 major nations (USA, Canada, Germany, France, Great Britain, Japan) from 1970-2020, identifying key population health patterns and outcomes.

## Tools Used
- Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
- Tableau Public

## Key Findings
- USA has highest healthcare spending but not highest life expectancy
- Japan achieves highest life expectancy with moderate spending
- All countries show consistent upward trend in healthcare spending over time
- Strong correlation between healthcare investment and life expectancy outcomes
